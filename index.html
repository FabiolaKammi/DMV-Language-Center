Welcome to DMV Language Center

New Approach to Language Learning

DMV Language Center is an innovative education company dedicated to empowering learners through multilingual education and future-ready skills.

We offer French, Spanish, Chinese, Vietnamese language programs through engaging, hands-on learning, online and on-site.

Our Programs

Multiple branches. One mission: Excellence.

Kids and Teens Group Classes

Our Kids and Teens Program offers structured language instruction in French, Spanish, Chinese, and Vietnamese. Each lesson builds foundational vocabulary, pronunciation, reading readiness, and age-appropriate communication skills.

Students are grouped by age and proficiency level to ensure proper developmental alignment. Learning is supported through guided activities, cultural immersion, interactive practice, and a progressive skill-building curriculum.

Adult Group Classes

The Adult Program offers comprehensive language instruction in French, Spanish, Chinese, and Vietnamese for learners at all proficiency levels.

Courses focus on practical communication, essential grammar, and cultural literacy. Adult learners receive systematic guidance, conversational practice, and skill-building exercises aligned with their personal or professional goals.

Private Classes

Our Private Classes offer personalized, one-on-one instruction in French, Spanish, Chinese, and Vietnamese. Lessons are customized to each student’s goals, pace, and learning style, providing focused support and faster progress.

This flexible option is ideal for students who need individualized attention, targeted practice, or specialized preparation.

Our Policies

Clear guidelines to ensure a smooth learning experience for all students.

Payment Policy

Tuition must be paid before the first class to secure a spot. Monthly and session-based payments are available. Late payments may result in paused enrollment.

Attendance Policy

Regular attendance ensures progress. Parents/students must notify us 24 hours in advance if they cannot attend. Missed classes are not refundable but may be rescheduled when possible.

Cancellation and Refunds

Cancellations made within 48 hours of the first class receive a full refund. After classes begin, refunds are not available, but credit may be applied to future programs.

Safety and Privacy

The safety of our students is our top priority. Personal information is kept private and used only for registration and communication. For minors, parents must provide emergency contact information.
